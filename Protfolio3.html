The Leafy Boutique - Plant Store Setup

Designed The Leafy Boutique for Danilo Santos, a stunning online plant shop built on Shopify. This project included a premium, mobile-friendly design, multi-page setup, optimized navigation, header & footer customization, and essential app integrations. I crafted a vibrant, nature-inspired aesthetic, enhanced site speed, and integrated secure payment gateways. Products were uploaded with high-quality images and descriptions to create a seamless shopping experience. Delivered a visually appealing, user-friendly store tailored to captivate plant lovers and boost online sales.
